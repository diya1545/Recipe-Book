Cassoulet

Description

Cooking Time

  • 10 Mins
  • 30 Mins
  • 310 Likes

Ingredients you need

  • 1 pound dried Great Northern beans
  • 2 ½ quarts unsalted chicken broth (10 cups)
  • 3 ounces salt pork
  • 2 duck confit legs
  • 8 ounces fresh French garlic sausage
  • 4 ounces belly
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Directions

  1. Soak the beans in salted water overnight.
  2. Simmer the beans, broth, salt pork, and duck confit until tender.
  3. Add garlic, sausage, belly, and pork skin. Simmer until flavors meld.
  4. Season with nutmeg, salt, and pepper.
  5. Transfer to a casserole dish, top with breadcrumbs, and bake until crusty
