Croissants

Description

Cooking Time

  • 10 Mins
  • 30 Mins
  • 310 Likes

Ingredients you need

  • 3 tablespoons warm water
  • 1 ¼ teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ⅔ cup warm milk
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • ⅔ cup unsalted butter (chilled)
  • 1 large egg

Directions

  1. Mix yeast, warm water, and 1 teaspoon of sugar. Let it foam.
  2. Combine flour, warm milk, 2 teaspoons of sugar, salt, yeast mixture, and oil. Knead until smooth.
  3. Let the dough rise until tripled in volume (about 3 hours).
  4. Deflate gently, let it rise again (about 3 hours).
  5. Chill the dough for 20 minutes.
  6. Pat the dough into a rectangle, smear chilled butter, fold, and chill.
  7. Repeat the folding process twice.
  8. Shape the croissants, let them rise, glaze with egg wash, and bake at 475°F (245°C) until golden brown.
