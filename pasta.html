Red Sauce Pasta

Description

Cooking Time

  • 10 Mins
  • 30 Mins
  • 310 Likes

Ingredients you need

  • 250g penne pasta
  • Chilli Powder
  • Oregano
  • 4 slices of mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon of crushed black pepper
  • 400g canned tomatoes (crushed or whole)
  • 2 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil
  • 1/2 cup grated ricotta salata cheese (or Parmesan)

Directions

  1. Slice the eggplants and sprinkle with salt. Let them sit for 30 minutes to remove excess moisture.
  2. Rinse the eggplant slices and pat them dry.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan and fry the eggplant slices until golden brown. Remove and set aside.
  4. In the same pan, saut√© minced garlic and add crushed tomatoes. Season with oregano, salt, and pepper.
  5. Cook the sauce until thickened.
  6. Cook the penne pasta according to package instructions.
  7. Toss the cooked pasta with the tomato sauce and fried eggplant slices.
  8. Serve with grated ricotta salata cheese and fresh basil leaves
