Sushi

Description

Cooking Time

  • 10 Mins
  • 30 Mins
  • 310 Likes

Ingredients you need

  • 6 sheets of sushi seaweed
  • 1 batch of prepared sushi rice
  • Â½ pound of sashimi-grade raw salmon
  • 4 ounces of cream cheese
  • 1 avocado (sliced)
  • Soy sauce (for serving)

Directions

  1. Place the seaweed on a bamboo mat.
  2. Cover the seaweed sheet with an even layer of prepared sushi rice.
  3. Smooth it gently with a rice paddle.
  4. Layer slices of salmon, cream cheese, and avocado on the rice.
  5. Roll it up tightly.
  6. Slice with a sharp knife.
  7. Enjoy your homemade sushi with soy sauce!
