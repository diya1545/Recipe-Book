Hazelnut Choco Cake

Description

Cooking Time

  • 10 Mins
  • 30 Mins
  • 310 Likes

Ingredients you need

  • Butter: 1 cup (unsalted, melted)
  • Cocoa Powder: 2/3 cup
  • White Sugar: 1 1/2 cups
  • Brown Sugar: 1 1/2 cups
  • Egg: 1 large
  • Vanilla Extract: 4 teaspoons
  • Baking Powder: 3/4 teaspoon
  • All-Purpose Flour: 2 2/3 cups

Directions

  1. Whisk the butter and cocoa powder: In a large bowl, whisk together the melted butter and cocoa powder.
  2. Add the sugar, egg, and vanilla: Stir in the white sugar, followed by the egg and vanilla extract.
  3. Mix in the dry ingredients and chocolate chips: Add the all-purpose flour, baking powder, and a pinch of salt. Then fold in the semisweet chocolate chips.
  4. Chill the dough: Refrigerate the dough for about 20 minutes.
  5. Preheat the oven: Set it to 350°F (180°C).
  6. Scoop and bake: Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a lined baking tray. Bake for approximately 10-12 minutes or until the edges are set but the centers remain soft.
  7. Enjoy!: Let the cookies cool slightly before indulging in their gooey, fudgy goodness.
  8. Nutella: You can customize these brownie cookies by adding your favorite mix-ins like nuts, Nutella, or sprinkles. Happy baking!
