Manchow Soup

Description

Cooking Time

  • 10 Mins
  • 30 Mins
  • 310 Likes

Ingredients you need

  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 inch ginger (finely chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 green chilies (chopped)
  • ½ onion (sliced)
  • ½ carrot (julienned)
  • 3 tablespoons cabbage (shredded)
  • ½ capsicum (diced)
  • 2 tablespoons coriander stems (chopped)
  • 4 cups water
  • ¾ teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)
  • Cornstarch (for thickening)
  • Crispy fried noodles (for garnish)

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add finely chopped garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Sauté for 30 seconds
  2. Add spring onion whites and sliced onions. Fry until the onions turn golden.
  3. Stir in shredded cabbage, chopped beans, diced bell peppers, julienned carrots, and chopped coriander stems. Stir-fry for a minute without overcooking the vegetables.
  4. Pour in 4 cups of water and add salt. Mix well.
  5. If you prefer a thicker consistency, you can add a slurry of cornstarch (cornflour) mixed with water.
  6. Serve the Manchow Soup hot, garnished with crispy fried noodles.
