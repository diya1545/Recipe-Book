Fafda

Description

Cooking Time

  • 10 Mins
  • 30 Mins
  • 310 Likes

Ingredients you need

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • ¼ teaspoon carom seeds (ajwain)
  • 10 to 12 black peppercorns (crushed coarsely)
  • ¼ teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 pinch asafoetida (hing)
  • ⅛ teaspoon baking soda (or 2 to 3 pinches of baking soda)
  • ¾ teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Water (as required)
  • Oil (for deep frying)

Directions

  1. n a bowl, mix the gram flour with carom seeds, crushed black pepper, turmeric powder, and asafoetida.
  2. Add baking soda (or papad khar if available) to the mixture.
  3. Gradually add water (about 2 to 3 tablespoons at a time) and knead into a tight dough.
  4. Incorporate 2 tablespoons of oil into the dough and continue kneading until you get a soft, smooth consistency.
  5. Cover the dough and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  6. After resting, divide the dough into small balls.
  7. On a lightly greased wooden board, flatten each ball into thin strips (fafda).
  8. Heat oil in a deep frying pan.
  9. Fry the fafda in batches until they turn golden brown and crispy.
  10. Remove from oil and drain on paper towels.
  11. Serve warm or at room temperature with fried green chilies, raw papaya sambharo, and besan chutney.
